Very Good Parrots

Large Parrots

Large parrots include the following:

  • Amazons
  • Cockatoos
  • Macaws
  • The Famously Intelligent African Grey
.

All of these can be trained to associate certain combinations of sounds (aka words!) with concepts they understand, like 'sleep' or 'food'. However, the bigger the animal, the bigger the space you need to care for it properly and ensure it has a happy life. This is doubly so for birds, as they are used to having large open spaces where they can exhaust their energy as they fly around.

Small Parrots

Smaller parrots are easier to care for, but generally not as smart. Many of their numbers are members of these species:

  • Budgerigars
  • Cockatiels
  • Conures
  • Parrotlets

Almost all of these smaller birds are more cuddly and loving towards their human when compared to the larger birds. Parrots are the most varied animal genus in terms of length.
